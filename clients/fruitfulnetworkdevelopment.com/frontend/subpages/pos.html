POS Options

Point-of-Sale Devices

Device options, integrations, and lock-in differences across providers.

We support a range of POS configurations depending on operational needs, scale, and connectivity. Our approach prioritizes simplicity, reliability, and alignment with existing payment and bookkeeping workflows.

In most cases, we recommend using PayPal's POS ecosystem as baseline. It integrates easily with other PayPal services, and there are no lock-in or price hikes for FND to develop custom product profiles and customer accounts — this is why PayPal's services allow us to offer unrestricted, competitive pricing.

Some systems are POS only, others are online payment processing only, and some are integrated solutions. See the web services comparison page for more details on web hosting options. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
