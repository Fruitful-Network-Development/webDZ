Program Overview

At Fruitful Network Development, we provide a simple, reliable payment foundation for community-driven enterprises. Whether you're raising funds under fiscal sponsorship or selling produce at market, our system keeps online donations, designated giving, and point-of-sale transactions coordinated under one operational framework.

Fiscal Sponsorship and Donation Types

Fiscal sponsorship is an arrangement where a recognized 501(c)(3) accepts donations and manages funds for mission-aligned projects that are not standalone charities. Donations are legally made to the sponsor, and the sponsor retains governance responsibility.

Donation handling generally includes unrestricted support, designated/restricted support, and conditional gifts tied to milestones. This structure allows projects to fundraise with accountability while sponsors maintain audit-ready controls and disbursement policy compliance.

Further Reading: Fiscal Sponsorship

Donation Operations

We recommend PayPal for most small organizations because it balances low-friction setup, donor familiarity, and strong transaction reporting. A single account can support multiple designated projects while maintaining clear internal separation.

The donation box module captures donor metadata (designation, comments, contact), automates receipt workflows, and keeps exports ready for bookkeeping, sponsor reporting, and review cycles.

Further Reading: Market Transparency

POS & In-Person Integration

Our approach can integrate in-person market-day payments with online donations, creating unified reporting while preserving correct separation between retail and charitable flows when required.

  • Unified reporting: align market transactions with online contributions.
  • Scalable workflows: use consistent operations across farms, events, and projects.
  • Clear separation: keep retail and charitable channels distinct for governance and accounting.
