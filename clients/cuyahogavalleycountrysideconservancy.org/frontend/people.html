Board member

Jennifer Gray

Jennifer Gray is the founder of JLG Law, LLC and brings extensive federal, state, and local trial experience to the board.

Read full profile

Jennifer is the founder of JLG Law, LLC, a solo practice that primarily provides criminal defense to individuals engaged in the federal, state, and local courts in the Cleveland area. She brings considerable experience in litigating complex criminal matters at the state and federal level, including matters involving multiple federal, state, and local law enforcement agencies. Before opening her own practice, Jennifer joined Flannery | Georgalis, LLC, as Counsel in the Cleveland office. She previously spent 18 months as a federal law clerk in the inaugural chambers of the Honorable Magistrate Judge James E. Grimes, Jr., of the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Ohio.

For the initial 12 years of her career, Jennifer was an Assistant District Attorney within the Special Victims, Investigations and Trial Divisions of the Bronx County District Attorney’s Office in New York, NY. She successfully prosecuted, investigated, and tried a wide array of high-profile criminal cases including homicides, sexual assaults, domestic violence matters, and numerous multiagency, long-term, and complex investigations. As a former Supervising Assistant District Attorney, Jennifer brings extensive experience in the assessment of cases, the anticipation of issues, the development of legal strategies for trials and hearings, and the written and oral motions practice in a fast-paced, high-volume, high-stakes environment.

During her time as a Bronx Assistant District Attorney working on Special Victims cases, Jennifer was trained as a forensic interviewer and has vast experience conducting and participating in forensic interviews, a skillset she now applies to clients and investigations in her private practice.
