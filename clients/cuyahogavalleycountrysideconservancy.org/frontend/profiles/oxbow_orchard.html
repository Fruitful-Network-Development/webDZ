Oxbow Orchard

6765 Canal Road, Valley View

Oxbow Orchard is a diversified, regenerative agriculture farm raising Icelandic sheep, pasture poultry (chickens and turkeys), seasonal fruits, vegetables, plant seedlings, and cut flowers. They operate a rustic farmstand and a small local foods shop on the property.
