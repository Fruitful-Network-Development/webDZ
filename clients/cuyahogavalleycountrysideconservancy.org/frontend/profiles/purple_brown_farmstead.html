Purple Brown Farmstead

Farm Location: 458 Hines Hill Road, Hudson
Shop Location: 1619 Mill St. W, Peninsula
Purplebrown Farmstead is a small, permaculture-oriented farm cultivating a resilient "food forest" of diverse crops. They grow heritage cider apples, various fruits and nuts, vegetables, herbs, flowers, and mushrooms, integrated with complementary animals. Products are also sold at an off-site farm store in downtown Peninsula.
