Neitenbach Farm

3077 Akron Peninsula Road, Akron

Neitenbach Farm uses biodynamic farming to grow mixed vegetables, culinary and medicinal herbs, and flowers. They sell produce through an on-farm stand and a unique CSA. Farmer Pamela Neitenbach also offers herbal tinctures, teas, and salves, along with holistic healing and bodywork services.
