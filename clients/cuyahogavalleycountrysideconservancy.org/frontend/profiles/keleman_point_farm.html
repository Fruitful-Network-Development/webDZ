Keleman Point Farm

4570 Akron Peninsula Rd, Peninsula, OH
Keleman Point Farm, a diversified sustainable farmstead (formerly “Goatfeathers Point Farm”), raises grass-fed beef, pork, heritage-breed turkeys (for Thanksgiving), Tennessee fainting goats (for cashmere fiber), and free-range eggs. Mike and Melissa Keleman took over the 48-acre farm in 2020, continuing its sustainable practices.
