Greenfield Berry Farm

2485 Major Road, Peninsula

Greenfield Berry Farm offers pick-your-own blueberries, aronia berries, and sunflowers, alongside a vegetable CSA program, pussy willows, tulips, and honey. Owner Daniel Greenfield, an environmental educator, also hosts farm-based educational tours and field trips.
