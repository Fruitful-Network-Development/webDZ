Trapp Family Farm

A family-run, mixed crop-and-livestock farm on Route 303 in Peninsula, inside Cuyahoga Valley National Park. Established 2012 through the park's Countryside Initiative,horse-powered, soil-focused, and open to the community.

What We Offer

  • Eggs , daily at the stand (while they last)
  • Produce , vegetables and fruit in season
  • Meat , pastured broilers, Thanksgiving turkeys, grass-fed lamb (pre-order; see Animal Husbandry)

Draft horses power the fieldwork. Healthy soil, healthy animals, and a place to learn where your food comes from.
