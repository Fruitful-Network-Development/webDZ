Wine List

Wine Style Notes
Blue Heron Blush Blush, Ohio table wine An estate blend of Vidal, Seyval, Cayuga, Traminette, and Rubiana grapes grown at Sarah’s Vineyard.
Cuyahoga Valley Reserve American red table wine A rich Red Zinfandel with hints of red cherry, aged in oak.
Double Trouble Ohio white wine A dry, balanced blend of Vidal and Traminette—clean and bright.
Golden Solstice Sweet Ohio table wine A fragrant sweet white made from Niagara grapes grown near Lake Erie.
Hampton Hills Chardonnay A dry and mellow white with hints of apple.
Irritablé American red wine A dry and fruity blend of Muscat, Alicante, and Zinfandel.
Miserabile American red table wine A Tuscan-style blend of Merlot, Sangiovese, and Rubiana.
Painted Lady Riesling, Ohio white table wine A dry Riesling that is fruity, crisp, and fresh tasting.
Sangria Sweet red sangria A crisp and refreshing summer blend—served over ice with fresh fruit.
Sweet Elisa Sweet Ohio table wine A “not-too-sweet” blend of Ohio Concord and Niagara.
