Blossom Music Center

Concert-day tips for a smoother visit.

  • Seating is first come, first served on concert days (no seating reservations).
  • Outdoor seating may be available—bring a lawn chair if there’s a wait.
  • Guests can order wine or beverages while waiting; to order food, you must be seated.
  • Food arrival times may vary between the pizza kitchen and main prep kitchen.
  • Prior to select concerts, we may offer a refined “Quick Bites” menu.
  • No outside food or alcohol (except cake/cupcakes). No tailgating in the parking lot.
  • No Blossom parking is permitted. Gates close at 10pm.

General Info

Helpful notes for everyone’s comfort and safety.

Gift certificates

Sold in store only. Available for any amount. Call to purchase by phone: (330) 929-8057.

Service

Table service is offered in the tasting room, deck, and butterfly garden. Bar service is offered at the pavilion and surrounding picnic tables.

Children

Children must be supervised and seated next to their parent at all times inside and outside.

Outside food & alcohol

No outside food is permitted. By Ohio law, all alcohol consumed must be purchased through Sarah’s Vineyard.
