Production Dorset Sheep

Our Flock

We raise production Dorset sheep on hill pastures in the Cuyahoga Valley, focusing on rotational grazing, forage quality, and animal health to deliver prime lamb and seed stock.

Grazing & Care

Rotational grazing

Dorsets move through small paddocks to maximize forage quality and soil health. Intensive rotation improves pasture recovery and animal condition.

Forage-focused · Soil-building

Feeding & health

Pasture-based diet with mineral supplementation; lambs finished on grass. Health program follows VCPR guidance to ensure well-being.

Animal welfare first
